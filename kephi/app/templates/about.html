Values



01
Sustainability

Environmentally conscious


We make major efforts to ensure that our technology operates under the strictest guidelines, using minimal natural and other resources, such as water and electricity, and leaving as small a footprint as possible. The philosophy behind our technology is to enable the green production of fish, using environmentally-friendly practices. We believe the field in which we operate will lead to profound social change, combining nutrition with values of sustainability.

02

Customer Success
Nurturing our partnerships


We build long-term relationships with our clients, because we understand that their success is measured not only in a working facility, but in a profitable one. In order to facilitate this, we provide ongoing support throughout the project and long after the facility is up and running.

03

Innovations
Pushing the boundaries


We invest vast resources to ensure we stand at the forefront of technology. We own two R&D centers where we conduct various studies using cutting-edge technologies, with new species of fish and seafood. This allows us to continue to offer our customers the most advanced solutions in the field.

04
Passion
It's in our nature


We work with conviction and great passion because we deeply believe that what we do will have a profound impact on the nutrition and health of future generations.
